What we do

Eight ways we deliver for your business.

From a single-sprint engagement to a multi-year managed partnership, People More covers the full stack of IT delivery. Choose a service line or talk to us — most engagements combine two or three.

01

IT Projects

For teams that need a defined deliverable, not an open-ended engagement.

Some projects have a clear start and a clear finish. A new product, a platform migration, a mobile application that doesn't exist yet. For these, People More takes full delivery ownership: scoping, architecture, build, test, and handover. You define the outcome; we define how to get there and hold ourselves accountable to it.

We staff projects with the engineers who match the domain, not whoever is available. Typical engagements run three to twelve months with a dedicated lead who acts as your single point of contact throughout.

  • Fixed deliverables with transparent milestone tracking
  • Full-stack capability: web, mobile, cloud, embedded, data
  • Architecture and code review included as standard
  • Handover with documentation and a 30-day support window
Case example

Primion. People More designed and built a visitor management system for a German security technology company, delivering a product used across their European client base. The project followed a fixed-scope model with phased delivery milestones.

02

Expanding Your Team

For in-house teams that need capacity, skills, or both — fast.

Hiring takes months. A good engineer from People More takes two to four weeks from first conversation to an active team member. They work in your tools, attend your standups, and report through your structure. From the outside, they look like your team — because they are.

Team extension works for one engineer or ten. We match on both technical skills and domain experience, so the ramp is short and the contribution starts early.

  • Senior-level engineers across web, mobile, cloud, data, QA, DevOps
  • Two to four week lead time from kick-off to active
  • Long-term availability: engineers committed to your product, not juggling clients
  • Direct management by your team, or via a People More lead if preferred
Case example

BT. People More embedded a cross-functional team with British Telecom's mobile product division to rebuild their iOS and Android apps as a single React Native codebase. Development cost was reduced by approximately 50% versus maintaining two native codebases.

03

Managed Applications

For businesses that need an ongoing engineering partner, not a vendor.

Your application does not stop needing attention after launch. New features, platform updates, compliance changes, performance issues — the work continues. Managed Applications gives you a standing team that knows your codebase and is accountable for its quality, evolution, and uptime over months and years.

This is not support ticket management. It is ongoing product engineering by a team that acts like a permanent part of your organization.

  • Ongoing feature development and continuous improvement cycles
  • Proactive monitoring, incident response, and SLA-backed availability
  • Platform upgrades and dependency management handled as a matter of course
  • Monthly reporting with roadmap planning included
Case example

Bosch. People More has served as Bosch's engineering partner for their connected-device platform for over ten years. The relationship has evolved with the product, scaling from four engineers to more than twelve across multiple feature domains.

04

Transformation Services

For organizations moving from legacy systems to modern platforms.

Legacy systems do not fail suddenly; they slow you down gradually until the cost of not changing becomes obvious. People More manages technology transformation as a structured, phased program: assess, plan, build, migrate, and stabilize. We have run migrations from on-premise to cloud, from monolith to microservices, and from aging codebases to modern stacks.

The goal is not to rebuild for rebuilding's sake. Every transformation decision is measured against business outcomes: faster delivery, lower operational cost, or unlocked capability.

  • Technology assessment and roadmap before any code is written
  • Phased migration to minimize disruption to live systems
  • Cloud architecture design and implementation (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Team upskilling included where the client team takes over post-migration
Case example

Primion. People More led a platform transformation for Primion's security management software, moving a Windows-native desktop application to a cloud-ready, browser-based architecture without disrupting their installed customer base.

05

Product Ideation & Creation

For founders and product leaders building something new from scratch.

Not every client arrives with a finished specification. Some arrive with a problem, a market insight, and a rough idea. Product Ideation and Creation is the service for that starting point: we run discovery workshops, define the product scope, prototype the core interactions, validate with users, and move directly into build.

By keeping discovery and delivery under one roof, there is no handover gap where assumptions are lost between the team that designed and the team that builds.

  • Structured product discovery: user research, competitive analysis, opportunity mapping
  • Rapid prototyping and concept validation before full investment
  • UX and UI design delivered alongside the engineering team, not ahead of it
  • MVP definition with a clear path from prototype to production
Case example

Vernaio GmbH. People More designed and built a full-stack IoT platform for industrial manufacturing clients, including a custom design system, frontend and backend modules, and a DevOps layer for their connected-device infrastructure.

06

Intelligent Automation & AI

For businesses ready to move from AI curiosity to AI in production.

AI is not a feature to add to your roadmap. It is an engineering discipline that requires the same rigor as any other: data quality assessment, model selection, integration architecture, evaluation frameworks, and ongoing monitoring. People More builds AI capabilities that work in real products, for real users, with observable performance.

We work with LLMs, computer vision, recommendation systems, and process automation — and we know how to connect them to the rest of your stack without making the rest of your stack a liability.

  • AI readiness assessment: data, infrastructure, and team capability
  • LLM integration and fine-tuning for domain-specific applications
  • Recommendation engines, classification, and prediction systems
  • Process automation with robust error handling and human-in-the-loop design
Case example

Kölner Stadt-Anzeiger. People More built a content recommendation engine for one of Germany's largest regional newspapers, improving editorial relevance and reducing editorial workload through intelligent content surface automation.

07

Professional Services

For teams that need specialist skills for a specific deliverable or audit.

Not every engagement requires a long-term team. Sometimes you need a security review before a major release. A QA pass on a codebase you inherited. A DevOps architect to design your CI/CD pipeline. Professional Services delivers specialist expertise at the scope you need, without the overhead of a full engagement.

These engagements are typically four to eight weeks, with a defined output: a report, a configuration, a process, or a handover package your in-house team can use.

  • QA strategy and test coverage for existing products
  • DevOps pipeline design and implementation (CI/CD, IaC, container orchestration)
  • Security review and penetration testing for pre-launch or post-incident response
  • Code quality audit with a prioritized remediation backlog
Case example

Squalify. People More delivered a structured QA and DevOps program for a recruitment technology platform, establishing automated test coverage and a reproducible deployment pipeline that reduced release cycle time significantly.

08

Project-based Consulting

For leaders who need senior thinking before committing to a direction.

Before you build, it is worth being certain that you are building the right thing the right way. Project-based Consulting brings senior People More engineers and architects into your decision-making process: reviewing vendor proposals, assessing your current architecture, defining technical requirements, or pressure-testing a roadmap before investment.

This is advisory work with a deliverable: a recommendation document, a technical specification, or a decision framework your team can act on independently.

  • Vendor and technology selection support with independent analysis
  • Architecture review and technical due diligence
  • Technical requirements definition for procurement or RFP processes
  • Roadmap review and prioritization workshops
Case example

DLL Group. People More provided advisory consulting to DLL's technology function, supporting vendor evaluation and requirements definition for a core financial platform replacement.
