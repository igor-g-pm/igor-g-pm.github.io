About People More

Built by engineers who wanted to do it differently.

People More is a software house headquartered in Kraków, Poland, with clients across Western Europe, the UK, and beyond. We were founded by former developers and designers who had enough experience building digital products to know exactly what clients and engineers actually need from a technology partner.

Our mission

"Realizing our clients' business dreams by effectively providing the best technology people."

People More grew out of Insignia, one of Poland's oldest and most established interactive agencies. That heritage means we understand both the craft side of software, how good products are actually built, and the business side, why they need to deliver.

Our founders made that transition themselves: from engineers and designers doing the work to leaders running a company. That perspective changes how we engage with clients, how we hire, and how we define success on every project.

What "People More" means

People determine outcomes

The quality of the people involved in an IT project, their communication, judgment, responsibility, and craft, determines whether the project succeeds. Technology choices matter, but the people making them matter more.

100% remote-friendly

Our office in Kraków is open for those who prefer it, but we have operated remote-first since the beginning. Our processes are built for asynchronous collaboration without losing quality or cohesion.

Flat hierarchy

Employees talk directly to the board. There are no layers between the people doing the work and the people who lead the company, which means decisions are faster, feedback is real, and nobody hides behind process.

Personal brand support

We actively help our engineers build visibility in the field: sponsoring conference talks, supporting magazine contributions, and giving people the space to be known for their expertise, not just their employer.
