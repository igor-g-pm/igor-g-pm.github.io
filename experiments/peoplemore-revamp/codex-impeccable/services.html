Core service lines

IT projects and bespoke software

We design and build applications from scratch or modernize specific modules in existing products.

  • Benefits: faster release cycles, architecture clarity, maintainable code base.
  • Best fit: companies launching new products or replacing legacy stacks.
  • Delivery cue: project-based with milestone planning.

Expanding your team

We provide dedicated specialists who integrate directly with your internal delivery and communication rhythms.

  • Benefits: quick scaling, specialist access, lower hiring pressure.
  • Best fit: CTOs who need immediate capacity for active roadmaps.
  • Delivery cue: dedicated team or staff augmentation.

Managed applications

We maintain, support, and improve business-critical applications with predictable service continuity.

  • Benefits: operational stability, lower downtime risk, continuous optimization.
  • Best fit: operations leaders managing live systems with tight SLA expectations.
  • Delivery cue: managed service with agreed response windows.

Transformation services

We help teams redesign aging architecture and migrate legacy components to modern technical foundations.

  • Benefits: lower maintenance cost, better performance, improved scalability.
  • Best fit: enterprises with fragmented or outdated systems.
  • Delivery cue: phased transformation roadmap.

Professional services

Specialized experts support targeted delivery areas such as QA, DevOps, cloud optimization, and integration work.

  • Benefits: faster problem resolution, deeper technical expertise, delivery resilience.
  • Best fit: teams needing focused skill support without full outsourcing.
  • Delivery cue: specialist assignment by sprint or workstream.

Product ideation and creation

We shape product direction through UX and technical planning, then move into build execution with the same team.

  • Benefits: clearer MVP scope, reduced rework, aligned product and engineering priorities.
  • Best fit: product teams validating new concepts under timeline pressure.
  • Delivery cue: discovery to implementation pipeline.

Project-based consulting

Our consultants support architecture decisions, process improvements, and delivery governance for complex initiatives.

  • Benefits: stronger planning confidence, reduced delivery risk, objective technical perspective.
  • Best fit: organizations preparing high-impact projects.
  • Delivery cue: scoped advisory engagement.

Intelligent automation and AI

We integrate automation and AI into business workflows, grounded in realistic data, process, and compliance constraints.

  • Benefits: streamlined operations, better decision support, scalable process improvements.
  • Best fit: teams modernizing operations with practical AI adoption.
  • Delivery cue: iterative automation roadmap.

Service FAQs

Can People More work with an existing in-house team?

Yes. Team extension and professional services are built for direct collaboration with internal product, engineering, and operations teams.

Do you handle both UX and engineering delivery?

Yes. People More combines UX/UI design with frontend, backend, mobile, and cloud delivery in one coordinated execution track.

Can we start with one project and then move to managed support?

Yes. Many engagements begin with a defined project and then transition into managed applications or long-term enhancement work.

Do you publish standard service pricing?

Not publicly available. Pricing depends on scope, team composition, timeline, and engagement model.
