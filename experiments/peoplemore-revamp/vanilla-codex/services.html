What We Offer

Services Built Around Product Outcomes and Team Fit

From end-to-end software delivery to targeted consulting and long-term application support, People More provides modular services that match your technical maturity and business pressure.

Service 1

IT Projects and End-to-End Development

We design and build digital products from scratch or modernize existing platforms while owning architecture, UX, engineering, and release.

Benefits

  • Single accountable team from discovery through launch.
  • Reduced handoff friction across design, frontend, backend, and QA.
  • Faster path from concept to market-ready product.
  • Architecture ready for scaling and integrations.

Fit and Pricing Signal

Target segments: SMEs launching new products, enterprises modernizing customer-facing systems.

Pricing signal: Project-based budgeting with fixed-scope milestones or mixed T&M sprint billing.

FAQ: How soon can development start?

Most projects start within 2-4 weeks after discovery and team alignment, depending on skill mix and environment readiness.

Service 2

Expanding Your Team (IT Outsourcing)

Access highly skilled European engineers who integrate with your internal workflows and delivery cadence.

Benefits

  • Fast capacity increase without long recruitment cycles.
  • Direct collaboration with dedicated specialists.
  • Knowledge transfer to internal teams.
  • Flexible scale-up and scale-down by roadmap phase.

Fit and Pricing Signal

Target segments: Product companies, scale-ups, and enterprise IT departments with ongoing backlogs.

Pricing signal: Monthly rate per specialist, typically under T&M or dedicated team contracts.

FAQ: Can augmented team members join daily standups?

Yes. We embed directly into your communication and project tools to operate as an extension of your team.

Service 3

Managed Applications

We maintain, optimize, and continuously improve business-critical systems under clear support and SLA expectations.

Benefits

  • Stable operations with proactive issue management.
  • Predictable support cost and response standards.
  • Less internal pressure on maintenance workloads.
  • Continuous quality improvements over time.

Fit and Pricing Signal

Target segments: Organizations with mature platforms that need reliability and evolutionary updates.

Pricing signal: Monthly managed-service retainers tied to environment complexity and SLA tier.

FAQ: Do you support legacy stacks?

Yes. Legacy support is often the first step before staged modernization to reduce business disruption.

Service 4

Intelligent Automation and AI

We introduce automation and AI into existing operations to reduce repetitive work and improve decision speed.

Benefits

  • Higher process efficiency and fewer manual errors.
  • Faster access to decision-grade operational insights.
  • Incremental rollout without full-system replacement.
  • Measurable productivity improvement opportunities.

Fit and Pricing Signal

Target segments: Mid-size and enterprise teams managing repetitive workflows or large operational datasets.

Pricing signal: Discovery-and-pilot model followed by phased implementation budgets.

FAQ: Can AI work with existing systems?

Yes. We prioritize API-based integration and low-risk pilots before scaling automation into core processes.

Service 5

Transformation Services

We help organizations move from legacy architectures and fragmented delivery to modern, scalable product ecosystems.

Benefits

  • Lower technical debt and better maintainability.
  • Improved release velocity across teams.
  • Clear modernization roadmap with business priorities.
  • Lower long-term operational cost.

Fit and Pricing Signal

Target segments: Enterprises and regulated organizations running mission-critical legacy systems.

Pricing signal: Architecture audit package plus phased modernization roadmap delivery.

FAQ: How do you reduce modernization risk?

Through staged migration, parallel validation, and clear rollback planning for critical business functions.

Service 6

Professional Services

Specialized experts provide targeted support in architecture, development, QA, DevOps, and product strategy.

Benefits

  • Access niche expertise only when needed.
  • Accelerated issue resolution for complex blockers.
  • Higher delivery quality through specialist oversight.
  • Lower risk in high-impact release phases.

Fit and Pricing Signal

Target segments: Teams needing short-term specialist intervention or audit support.

Pricing signal: Day-rate or sprint-based advisory assignments.

FAQ: Is this only for short engagements?

No. Professional services can start as targeted consulting and evolve into managed or dedicated collaboration.

Service 7

Product Ideation, Nurture, and Creation

From opportunity framing to UX and MVP delivery, we help shape products that are technically feasible and market relevant.

Benefits

  • Better product-market alignment before heavy investment.
  • Cross-functional discovery combining business and technical views.
  • Faster MVP cycle with reduced rework risk.
  • Stronger UX consistency across channels.

Fit and Pricing Signal

Target segments: New ventures, innovation units, and companies launching new digital offerings.

Pricing signal: Workshop-led discovery packages and MVP milestone billing.

FAQ: Do you support only design or full build too?

We can deliver discovery-only outcomes or continue into full engineering and managed release support.

Service 8

Project-Based Consulting

We help unblock critical initiatives with consulting-led delivery support grounded in practical implementation.

Benefits

  • Quick diagnosis of architecture and delivery bottlenecks.
  • Actionable roadmap tied to measurable milestones.
  • Reduced project uncertainty for stakeholders.
  • Option to transition smoothly into execution support.

Fit and Pricing Signal

Target segments: Leadership teams making high-stakes platform or process decisions.

Pricing signal: Fixed-fee consulting packages by scope and strategic depth.

FAQ: What does a consulting engagement deliver?

Usually an assessment report, prioritized action plan, risk map, and a recommended delivery model for implementation.
