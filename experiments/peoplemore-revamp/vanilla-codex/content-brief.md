### Accessibility + Mobile-First Notes
- Maintain AA color contrast for text and interactive components.
- Use semantic heading hierarchy (single H1 per page; ordered H2/H3 blocks).
- Provide visible focus styles for links, buttons, and form controls.
- Ensure tap target minimums of 44px on mobile CTAs.
- Keep forms keyboard accessible with explicit labels and required-field indicators.
- Avoid dense text walls; use short paragraphs and card-based grouping.

### Prioritized QA Checklist (Content + Launch)
1. Validate all SEO meta title/description character lengths and uniqueness per page.
2. Verify legal/compliance copy for privacy consent and public contact publication.
3. Confirm all client names, quotes, and metrics have approval for external usage.
4. Validate all CTA links and anchor jumps on desktop and mobile.
5. Run accessibility checks (contrast, keyboard navigation, form labels, focus order).
6. Confirm responsive behavior for hero sections, matrix tables, and forms.
7. Proofread for consistency of tone, terminology, and punctuation across pages.
8. Validate analytics events for CTA clicks and form submissions.
9. Confirm page performance targets (image sizes, font loading strategy).
10. Execute final stakeholder sign-off on messaging and positioning.
