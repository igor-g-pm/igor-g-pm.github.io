Frequently asked

Things prospective clients usually ask.

Are you the right size for our project? +
We work with everyone from early-stage SMEs to global corporations. Engagements scale up or down — from a single specialist to a full cross-functional team — without changing partner.
What technologies do you specialize in? +
Frontend in React, Angular and TypeScript. Backend in .NET, Java, Python, Node.js or Erlang. Mobile across all major platforms. Specialist work in embedded firmware, low-code (Pebble), and cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP).
How do engagements typically start? +
A free 30-minute call to understand your problem. If we're a fit, we propose a short paid discovery sprint that ends with a written plan, cost estimate and timeline — yours to keep regardless of whether we work together.
Do you sign NDAs and work with regulated industries? +
Yes. We routinely operate under NDA and have shipped work in finance, healthcare and physical security. Compliance and audit-trail requirements can be designed in from the first sprint.
Where are your developers based? +
Across Europe, with our HQ in Kraków, Poland. We work remote-first and accommodate client time zones across EMEA and the Americas.
