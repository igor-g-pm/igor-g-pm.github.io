Our Origin

From Insignia's DNA
to a New Vision

People More is a direct offshoot of Insignia — one of Poland's oldest and most respected interactive agencies. We inherited decades of hard-won knowledge in how digital products work in the real world. Then we built something new with it.

What makes us different: our founders wrote production code and shipped real products before they led companies. They understand technical trade-offs not from a slide deck, but from having made them under deadline pressure themselves.

The result is a company with a flat hierarchy, where developers talk directly to the board, where 100% remote work is a reality not a benefit, and where people are genuinely helped to grow — through conference speaking, publications, and projects that stretch their capabilities.
