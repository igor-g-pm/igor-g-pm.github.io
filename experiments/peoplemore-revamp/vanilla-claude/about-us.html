Who We Are

Our Purpose

Our Mission

"Realizing our clients' business dreams by effectively providing the best technology people."

This is not a tagline built in a marketing meeting. It is a direct statement of how we measure success: a client's project lands on time, their platform scales, their team grows — and the people who made that happen feel proud of what they built.

Our Vision

We believe the best software partnerships are built on three things: technical excellence, honest communication, and genuine care for the people on both sides of the contract. Our vision is to be the IT partner that European businesses trust not just for one project, but for every phase of their growth.

Where We Come From

Rooted in 20+ Years of Polish IT Excellence

People More did not start from zero. We are an offshoot of Insignia — one of Poland's oldest and most established interactive agencies — and we carry forward more than two decades of experience in digital media, telecommunications, and industrial software.

What makes our origin story unusual is who founded us. The people behind People More are not professional managers who spotted a market opportunity. They are former software developers and designers — people who have personally written the code, reviewed the pull requests, and sat in the late-night debugging sessions. That background shapes everything: how we structure teams, how we communicate with clients, and how we evaluate the quality of the work we deliver.

The Name Is the Philosophy

The company name "People More" reflects a deliberate double meaning. For clients, it means access to more skilled people — the engineers and designers who will move their projects forward. For our employees, it means being treated as more than a resource: as professionals whose wellbeing, growth, and voice matter. Both readings are equally true, and equally important to us.

Our Difference

What Sets Us Apart

Founders Who Code

Our leadership team comes from engineering and design backgrounds. When a client describes a technical challenge, our management team understands it viscerally — not just commercially. This reduces translation loss between business goals and technical execution.

Flat Hierarchy, Direct Access

At People More there is no labyrinth of account managers between you and the people making decisions. Employees and clients alike can speak directly with the board. This means faster decisions, greater accountability, and a partnership that actually feels like one.

Remote-First, Always Available

We operate 100% remote-friendly, with an office in Kraków for those who prefer in-person collaboration. Our teams are distributed but coherent, and our communication infrastructure ensures that being distributed never means being out of sync.

Brand Builders, Not Just Code Suppliers

We invest in our people's professional visibility. People More sponsors conference speaking engagements and magazine publications for its developers. This attracts the kind of engineers who care about their craft — and those are exactly the people you want building your product.

Our Culture

How We Work, Day to Day

At People More, culture is not a slide in an onboarding deck. It shows up in how we respond when a sprint goes sideways, how we handle a client who changes requirements, and how we treat a junior developer who asks a question for the third time.

We are direct without being unkind. We are ambitious without burning people out. We celebrate what ships, and we fix what breaks — together.
