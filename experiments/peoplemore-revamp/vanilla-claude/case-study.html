Primion — Legacy Stack Modernisation

Access Control & Security Technology • Germany

PRI

Primion Technology AG

Full-platform legacy modernisation for a leader in access control & security technology

Germany Access Control & Security
The Challenge

Primion's core platform had grown over many years, built on legacy architecture and outdated mobile applications developed by multiple external vendors. As their customers demanded more modern features, better performance, and a consistent user experience across devices, the platform was struggling to keep pace.

  • Auditing and understanding a large, aging, multi-vendor codebase
  • Delivering new applications aligned with a newly defined architecture
  • Ensuring fast, predictable delivery of business-critical features
  • Reducing mounting maintenance costs
  • Preparing the platform for future scalability and innovation

The stakes were high: Primion's products are used in physical access control and security environments where reliability is non-negotiable.

Our Solution

People More took on full responsibility as Primion's technology partner — not just for individual components, but for the architectural vision and delivery continuity across the entire modernisation programme. Our approach centred on four pillars:

  • Full System Audit & Architecture Redesign — Comprehensive assessment of the existing platform, mapped dependencies, and designed a new microservices-based architecture.
  • Application Re-development & New Feature Delivery — Rebuilt core applications using modern frameworks (Java backend, Angular web, Flutter mobile), ensuring feature parity with the legacy system.
  • UX/UI Refresh & Cross-Platform Consistency — Redesigned the user experience to deliver a consistent, modern interface across desktop and mobile touchpoints.
  • Continuous Delivery & Managed Services — Implemented CI/CD pipelines and established a managed services arrangement to maintain the platform post-launch.
The Process
  • Discovery and audit: comprehensive review of existing codebase, architecture, and mobile applications
  • Architecture design: new microservices blueprint agreed with Primion's technical leadership
  • Parallel development tracks: new applications developed alongside live legacy system to minimise disruption
  • Flutter mobile development: unified cross-platform mobile app replacing multiple vendor-built legacy apps
  • CI/CD pipeline implementation: automated build, test, and deployment infrastructure established
  • Knowledge transfer and documentation: full handover materials prepared for Primion's internal team
Delivered Value
Faster delivery of critical new functionalities — modern CI/CD pipelines and a clean architecture reduced time-to-feature
Significant reduction in development and maintenance costs — a single, unified codebase replaced fragmented multi-vendor legacy code
Single point of responsibility — Primion now has one partner accountable for the full technology stack
Modern, scalable platform prepared to support Primion's roadmap for the next 5+ years
