Managed Applications

We take operational responsibility for your applications under a defined Service Level Agreement. Your systems stay available, performant, and up to date — so your team can focus on building, not firefighting.

  • Guaranteed uptime — SLA-backed commitments with defined response and resolution times, so you know exactly what to expect when something goes wrong.
  • Proactive monitoring — We detect and resolve issues before they become outages, using continuous monitoring and alerting across your application stack.
  • Predictable costs — Fixed monthly retainers replace unpredictable incident-driven spending, making financial planning straightforward.
  • Single point of responsibility — One partner owns the outcome. No finger-pointing between vendors, no gaps in ownership.
Best for
  • Mid-market and enterprise clients with production applications that cannot afford downtime
  • Companies that have completed a build phase and need a reliable operations partner
  • Businesses transitioning from in-house ops teams to an outsourced managed services model
Our SLAs define uptime guarantees, incident response times (typically 1-hour acknowledgement for critical issues), resolution targets, and reporting cadence. We tailor the specific terms to your application's criticality and your business operating hours. We will walk you through the full SLA structure during our initial scoping conversation.

Transformation Services

We help businesses modernise legacy systems and undergo digital transformation — without halting business operations or accumulating further technical debt. From architecture audits to full re-platforming, we manage complexity so you do not have to.

  • Legacy to modern without disruption — Phased migration strategies keep your existing systems live while we rebuild beneath them, reducing operational risk.
  • Architecture audit and redesign — We assess your current technical landscape objectively and design a future-state architecture aligned with your business goals.
  • Reduced maintenance overhead — Modern, well-structured systems cost less to operate, patch, and extend than aging monoliths.
  • Scalable foundations — We build for where your business is going, not just where it is now.
Best for
  • Enterprises operating on systems built 5–15+ years ago that are constraining growth
  • Companies whose vendor landscape has fragmented over time
  • Organisations preparing for a new product strategy that the current platform cannot support
We begin with a full system audit to understand your current state and dependencies, then design a phased migration plan that keeps critical functionality live throughout. We use techniques like strangler-fig patterns, feature flags, and blue-green deployments to de-risk each transition step. You approve the plan before any migration work begins.

Professional Services

We place specialist IT consultants and engineers — individually or in small teams — into your organisation for defined assignments. Whether you need a senior architect for three months or a QA specialist for a product launch, we find and deploy the right person quickly.

  • Rapid placement — We draw from our existing network of vetted specialists; typical time-to-placement is significantly faster than independent recruitment.
  • Exact-fit expertise — We match not just technical skills but seniority level, domain experience, and communication style to your specific context.
  • No long-term commitment required — Engage for a defined period, then extend, reduce, or conclude without the overhead of permanent employment.
  • Full transparency on specialist credentials — You meet and assess candidates before any commitment is made.
Best for
  • Companies with a skills gap for a specific project phase (e.g., a data engineering specialist for a migration)
  • Businesses supplementing an existing team with a senior architect or tech lead
  • Organisations needing specialist QA, DevOps, or security expertise on a time-bound basis
We stand behind our placements. If, within the first two weeks, either party identifies a mismatch — technical or interpersonal — we work to resolve it, including replacing the specialist if necessary. Our reputation depends on placements that actually work.

  • Structured ideation — We facilitate discovery workshops that turn business ambitions into defined product visions, user stories, and technical requirements.
  • UX-first design — Our founders' design background runs through every product we help create. Interfaces are built for real users, not for feature lists.
  • End-to-end delivery capability — We can take a product from concept through design, development, testing, and launch without switching partners.
  • Ongoing "nutrition" — Post-launch, we continue evolving the product based on user feedback and market signals, keeping it competitive and healthy.
Best for
  • Startups and scale-ups launching their first or second digital product
  • Corporate innovation teams building a new digital service outside their core platform
  • Businesses with a product idea but no internal team to execute it

Project-based Consulting

Sometimes you need expert thinking before you need execution. Our project-based consulting engagements deliver targeted advisory — technology strategy, architecture review, vendor selection, or digital roadmap planning — on a defined scope and timeline.

  • Objective technical perspective — An outside view from senior engineers who have seen the same problems across many industries and scales.
  • Actionable deliverables — Every consulting engagement ends with documented recommendations you can act on — not a slide deck that sits on a shelf.
  • Pre-build validation — Validate your technology choices, architecture decisions, or build-versus-buy analysis before committing significant investment.
  • Flexible scope — Engagements can be as focused as a one-week architecture review or as broad as a multi-month digital transformation roadmap.
Best for
  • CTOs and technical leaders facing a major technology decision (re-platform, cloud migration, AI adoption)
  • Boards and executive teams needing an independent technology audit before a major investment
  • Companies evaluating whether to build, buy, or partner for a new capability
Consulting engagements vary widely. Common formats include a two-week architecture review producing a written assessment and recommendations, a four-week technology strategy sprint aligned to a specific business objective, or an ongoing advisory retainer for a fixed number of hours per month. We scope all engagements collaboratively in an initial discovery call.

Expanding Your Team

We extend your internal engineering team with vetted Polish IT specialists who integrate directly into your workflow, tools, and culture. Unlike a traditional outsourcing model, your augmented team members report to you, communicate directly with your team, and are held to your standards — not ours.

  • Fast ramp-up — We can place vetted engineers within 2–4 weeks, compared to 3–6 months for a conventional hire through a recruitment agency.
  • Direct integration — Augmented team members join your stand-ups, use your tools (Jira, Slack, GitHub), and contribute as full team members — not as an offshore "separate track."
  • Scalable team composition — Add one engineer or ten, across multiple specialisations, with the flexibility to adjust as your project evolves.
  • Cost-competitive European talent — Poland's engineering talent pool offers a compelling balance of technical excellence and competitive rates, without the timezone challenges of true offshore models.
Best for
  • Scale-ups that need to double their engineering velocity quickly without a 6-month hiring cycle
  • Enterprises filling skills gaps (e.g., a React or Java specialist) on active projects
  • Western European companies seeking nearshore partners in compatible timezones
The critical difference is accountability. Freelancers from marketplaces are self-managing and their quality is variable. Our augmented team members are People More employees — vetted, supported, and backed by our management. If a team member is underperforming, we address it. You get the flexibility of external resource with the reliability of a managed partnership.
